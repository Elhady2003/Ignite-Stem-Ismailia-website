What is Ignite?!

Ignite (Ignite Talks) is a series of events where speakers have five minutes to talk on a subject accompanied by 20 slides, for 15 seconds each, automatically advanced, forcing the speaker to get to the point swiftly, for the sake of saving the audience’s Duracell battery of thoughts and enhance them with more pioneering ideas ignite, Where you can simultaneously save time and effort sharing your impressive, exhilarating ideas on a magnificent platform only for ambitious, scrupulous sort of beings.
